Recipe for Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled eggs are a great breakfast food. Here's my recipe.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 2 T. butter
  • 2 T. cream

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Whisk them around.
  3. Pour eggs into the pan.

Enjoy with toast.
